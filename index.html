- Introduction -

A howl through the environment…

As humanity we find ourselves in a moment of extreme need to protect and conserve ecosystems and biodiversity. This is a sociocultural and economic problem of the Anthropocene that degrades the environmental goods and services of ecosystems.

“Howler Carayas Club" is a collection of 2772 NFTs of profile picture (Profile picture NFTs - PFP NFTs) with DeFi (Decentralized Finance) and Blockchain that allows the creation of a transparent economic tool for non-governmental organizations and/or other citizen science initiatives with existing and consolidated programs in terms of biological conservation of the Howler Monkey (Genus: Alouatta) in Latin America.

Currently, the species of the genus are in some category of the Red List of Threatened Species of the International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN-Red List, for its acronym in English). These species are ecologically important for the optimal sustainability of biodiversity and the health of the ecosystems in which they inhabit.

- Objectives -

1. Raise funds for howler monkey habitat reserves

2. Create a financing model for projects aimed at environmental conservation

3. Demonstrate that it is possible to generate economic returns by creating and caring for protected areas

4. Establish alliances with organizations involved in the creation and maintenance of protected natural areas

5. unite and create a community committed to caring for the ecosystem

6. Set precedents in the possibility of solving reserves and environmental causes through NFTs using carbon neutral blockchains

7. Provide new solutions to make protected nature reserves development tools for communities neighboring the reserves

8. Make visible the need for the creation and maintenance of natural protected areas for the development of wildlife

9. Provide information related to the conservation status of LATAM reserves
